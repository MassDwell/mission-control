## Massachusetts ADU Law (Key Points)

- ADUs allowed by-right in single-family zones (as of 2024)
- Max 900 sqft or 50% of principal dwelling
- One ADU per lot (plus one JADU in some cases)
- Owner-occupancy requirements vary by town
- Setback requirements vary
