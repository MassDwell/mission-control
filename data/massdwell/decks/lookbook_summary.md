## Company Overview

**Tagline:** "Where Life Fits"

MassDwell is a modular housing company based in Massachusetts, specialized in delivering high-quality ADUs through precision off-site construction. Combines smart design, fast delivery, and regulatory expertise.

**Target Customers:**
1. **Homeowners** — Add independent unit, host family, earn rental income
2. **Rental Investors** — Boost portfolio value with fast, high-quality ADU installations
3. **Small Lot Builders** — Turnkey modular solutions that cut time and cost
