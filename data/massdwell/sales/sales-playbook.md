## Objection Handling

### "Too Expensive"
**Response:** "I understand budget is a concern. What price point would work for you? We have options starting at $141K for our Essential model. Plus, many clients use HELOCs which spread the cost over time while the ADU generates rental income. Would you like to see the numbers on how that could work for your situation?"

### "Not ready yet"
**Response:** "Totally understand. Can you share what's holding you back? Is it timing, budget, or something else? I want to make sure I'm providing helpful information for whenever you are ready."

### "Need to talk to spouse/family"
**Response:** "Of course. Would it be helpful if I sent some materials you can share with them? Or we could schedule a call where everyone can ask questions together."

### "Permitting seems complicated"
**Response:** "That's actually one of our specialties. We handle the entire permitting process for you. In fact, in [their town], we've already completed [X] projects. Would it help to hear how smooth that process was for one of your neighbors?"

### "What about resale value?"
**Response:** "Great question. ADUs in Massachusetts typically add 20-35% to property value. We can show you comps in your area. Plus, the rental income potential means the ADU often pays for itself."
