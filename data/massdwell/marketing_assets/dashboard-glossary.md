# MassDwell Marketing Dashboard - Glossary of Metrics

## 1. Overview

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Reach** | Number of unique people who viewed the content or ad |
| **Impressions** | Total times ads were displayed, regardless of clicks |
| **Leads** | Total leads generated (people who expressed interest) |
| **Clicks** | Total clicks on the ads |
| **Cost Per Lead (CPL)** | Average cost per lead = Total investment ÷ Leads |
| **Cost Per Click (CPC)** | Average cost per click = Total spend ÷ Clicks |

## 2. Meta Ads

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Amount Spent** | Total money spent on Meta Ads campaigns |
| **Leads** | Number of leads generated by campaigns |
| **Clicks** | Total clicks on ads |
| **Reach** | Unique people who viewed ads |
| **Impressions** | Total times ads were shown |
| **Cost Per Lead (CPL)** | Average cost to generate a lead |
| **Cost per Link Click** | Average cost per click on a link within the ad |
| **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** | Percentage of clicks relative to impressions |
| **% Conversions Leads** | Percentage of conversions (leads) from ad interactions |
| **Best Ads (Leads)** | Ads that generated the most leads |

## 3. Google Ads

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Cost** | Total amount spent on Google Ads campaigns |
| **Conversions** | Specific actions performed by users (form submissions, purchases) |
| **Cost per Conversion** | Average cost per conversion = Total spend ÷ Conversions |
| **Clicks** | Total clicks on Google Ads |
| **CPC (Cost per Click)** | Average cost per click = Total spend ÷ Clicks |
| **Keywords** | Words associated with ads and their performance |
| **Conversions per Gender** | Distribution of conversions by gender |

## 4. Google Analytics (Website)

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Active Users** | Unique users who interacted with the website |
| **Event Count** | Total tracked events (button clicks, page views) |
| **New Users** | First-time visitors to the website |
| **Key Events** | Most important events (conversion actions, key interactions) |
| **Engagement Rate** | % of sessions with meaningful interactions |
| **Pages** | Number of page views |
| **Devices** | Traffic distribution by device type |
| **Origin of Access** | Geographic location of users |
| **Engaged Sessions** | Sessions with meaningful interactions |

---

## Key Formulas

- **CPL** = Total Spend ÷ Number of Leads
- **CPC** = Total Spend ÷ Number of Clicks
- **CTR** = (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100
- **Cost per Conversion** = Total Spend ÷ Conversions
- **Engagement Rate** = Engaged Sessions ÷ Total Sessions
